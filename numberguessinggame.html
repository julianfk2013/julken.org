Game Rules

Here are the rules of the Number Guessing Game:

  • Guess a number between 1 and 100.
  • If you guess the number correctly within 5 tries, you earn a point.
  • You can use the earned points to get hints.
  • There are two types of hints available:
    • General Area: This hint tells you whether the number is higher or lower than 50.
    • Detailed Range: This hint provides a specific range to narrow down your guess.
  • Each hint costs a certain number of points.
  • If you run out of points, you won't be able to use hints.
  • Good luck and have fun!
